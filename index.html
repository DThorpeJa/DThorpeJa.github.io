What is Bidsquawk?

An open social platform for services where anyone can be a freelancer from the hobbyist to the professional, bidding for job/projects. This platform will promote good business, competitive rates and more options for both customers and freelancers.

How is BidSquawk different?

Our open social platform will allow customers and freelancers to engage each other in a more social & productive way. Our platform will allow customers to offer physical in person jobs along with online remote jobs for everyone and there are no restrictions for payment methods whether it be through our online escrow service, cash in hand or any other medium agreed upon by both parties.
